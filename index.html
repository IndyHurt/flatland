How tall are the buildings in your area?

Welcome to our Targeted Editing series where today you can explain how the universe is expanding, or make OpenStreetMap data outstanding! (both take about the same amount of time)

  1. How can building heights be used?

    1. Help create 2.5D visualizations. First of all, what is 2.5D? It looks just like 3D, but it has no overhangs. The world is not flat according to most people. When buildings have heights associated with them, amazing visualizations can be built!

    2. Contribute to shade models. A tall building sure does throw a lot of shade, but you will never know how much if you don't have some idea of how tall a building is.

    3. Contribute to line of sight models. How's the view? When all of the buildings are flat, you can see for days! That's probably not what you want.
